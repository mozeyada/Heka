# Beta Acquisition Plan - Heka

## Executive Summary

**Goal:** Acquire 50-100 beta users in Australia before beta launch (Week 10-12)  
**Strategy:** Organic-first approach with minimal paid advertising  
**Budget:** $200-500  
**Timeline:** Start Week 6 (4 weeks before beta launch)

---

## Target Audience

### Primary Demographics
- **Age:** 16-45 years
- **Location:** Australia (focus on Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane)
- **Relationship Status:** Established couples (6+ months together)
- **Pain Points:** 
  - Recurring arguments/disagreements
  - Communication challenges
  - Seeking affordable relationship help
  - Tech-savvy, comfortable with apps

### User Personas

**Persona 1: "Struggling Millennials"**
- Age 25-35
- Relationship 1-5 years
- Career-focused, busy lifestyles
- Arguments about time, priorities, finances
- Active on Instagram, Facebook, Reddit

**Persona 2: "Growing Couples"**
- Age 20-30
- Relationship 6 months - 2 years
- Moving in together / serious commitment
- Arguments about values, future plans, communication styles
- Active on TikTok, Instagram, relationship forums

## Acquisition Channels

### Channel 1: Social Media (Organic) - PRIMARY
**Platforms:**
- **Facebook:** Relationship groups, couples communities
- **Instagram:** Relationship wellness accounts, couple influencers
- **Reddit:** r/relationships, r/AusRelationships, r/CouplesTherapy
- **TikTok:** Relationship content creators

**Strategy:**
1. Join relevant Facebook groups (10-15 groups)
2. Share valuable relationship advice (not promotional)
3. When appropriate, mention Heka as helpful tool
4. Create engaging relationship content on Instagram
5. Engage with relationship TikTok creators (comment, collaborate)

**Content Ideas:**
- "5 signs your argument is actually about something deeper"
- "How to have productive arguments (not destructive ones)"
- "Red flags vs. normal relationship challenges"
- Relationship communication tips
- Behind-the-scenes of building Heka (authentic storytelling)

**Timeline:** Weeks 6-14 (ongoing)

## Beta Sign-Up Process

### Landing Page Requirements
1. **Clear Value Proposition**
   - "Resolve arguments and build a stronger relationship"
   - "7-day free trial, no credit card required"

2. **Social Proof** (even if limited)
   - "Join 50+ couples already using Heka"
   - Early user testimonials (if available)

3. **Simple Sign-Up Form**
   - Email
   - Password
   - Age confirmation (16+)
   - Location (Australia)

4. **What Happens Next**
   - "Beta launching in [date]"
   - "You'll get early access + special pricing"

### Email Sequences

**Welcome Email (Immediate):**
- Thank you for interest
- Beta timeline
- What to expect
- Social sharing incentive

**Pre-Launch Email (Week 12):**
- Beta launching soon
- Reminder of value proposition
- Excitement building

**Launch Email (Week 13):**
- Beta is live!
- Access instructions
- Feedback request

## Risks & Mitigation

### Risk 1: Low Sign-Up Rate
**Mitigation:**
- Start early (Week 6)
- Multiple channels
- Personal network leverage
- Adjust messaging based on early feedback

### Risk 2: Wrong Audience
**Mitigation:**
- Clear targeting (established couples, 16+)
- Messaging focused on relationship challenges
- Feedback loops to refine targeting

### Risk 3: Budget Constraints
**Mitigation:**
- Focus on organic channels first
- Small paid tests ($50-100)
- Prioritize high-impact activities
