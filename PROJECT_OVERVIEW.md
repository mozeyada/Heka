# Heka - AI-Powered Couple Argument Resolution Platform

## Vision
Heka is an AI-powered application designed to help couples resolve disagreements and arguments in a healthy, constructive manner. The platform uses advanced AI to mediate discussions, provide unbiased perspectives, and guide couples toward mutually beneficial solutions.

## Project Status
**Phase:** Planning & Design  
**Date:** Initial Planning Phase

---

## Core Problem Statement
Couples often struggle to resolve arguments effectively due to:
- Emotional barriers preventing clear communication
- Lack of neutral perspective
- Difficulty identifying underlying issues
- Inability to find compromise
- Escalation cycles that worsen conflicts

## Solution Approach
Heka will act as both a **proactive relationship health platform** and **reactive AI mediator** that:
- **Proactively:** Helps couples maintain relationship health through check-ins, goals, and exercises
- **Preventively:** Identifies potential issues before they become arguments
- **Reactively:** Provides neutral, unbiased analysis of both perspectives when conflicts arise
- Identifies root causes and patterns
- Suggests constructive communication strategies
- Offers potential solutions and compromises
- Tracks progress and relationship health over time

**Positioning:** "Build a stronger relationship and resolve conflicts when they arise"
